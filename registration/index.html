Senior School Certificate Examination (SSCE)

The Senior School Certificate Examination (SSCE) Internal, is the examination conducted for candidates at the end of their Secondary School Education and the SSCE External is conducted for candidates that have attempted and have deficiency(ies) to make-up.

Internal External

Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE)

The Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE) is the examination conducted for candidates of Federal Unity Colleges and other allied institutions who have completed their Junior Secondary School education.

Learn More

National Common Entrance Examination (NCEE)

This National Common Entrance Examination (NCEE) is administered to pupils in their 6th year of primary schooling which is the final stage of primary education. The purpose of this examination is to select the qualified candidates from every state of the federation and the Federal Capital Territory (FCT) for admission into Federal Unity colleges.

Learn More

National Gifted Examination (NGE)

The National Gifted Examination (NGE) programme is designed to identify exceptionally talented children at the primary school level, bringing them together in the same environment at the secondary school level.
